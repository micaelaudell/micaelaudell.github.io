MY FAVORITE FOODS

FAVORITE BREAKFAST

I love to make avocado toast. My favorite is sour bread toast with avocado mashed with lemon, feta cheese and balsamic on top. I also love dipping it in hot sauce on the side!

FAVORITE LUNCH

My favorite food for lunch is usually grilled cheese. It’s the best kid food that I eat all the time. I also always dip it in ketchup, even though most people hate the taste of that.

FAVORITE DINNER

My favorite dinner food is penne alle vodka. My favorite recipe is from famous model Gigi Hadid. I always make this for dinner at home, it’s super easy and delicious!

FAVORITE DESSERT

My favorite desert would definitely be a Nutella crepe. I have my own homemade recipe that I make all the time. I sometimes like to add strawberries too.

FAVORITE SNACK

My favorite snack is Extra Cheddar Goldfish. I prefer this flavor over the regular one because it is tastes more like cheese. It may be messy with the crumbs, but it is tasty!

FAVORITE SMOOTHIE

My favorite smoothie is strawberry banana. I also sometimes like blackberries and raspberries too, but I prefer to have just strawberries and bananas. It is sweet and healthy!
